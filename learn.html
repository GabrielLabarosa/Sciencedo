Salcedo's Interactive Website

This website encourage sustainability for a safer environment whilst showcasing the talents and creativity of section Salcedo.

Who are we?

We are Grade 11-Salcedo

Overview

Grade 11 – Salcedo under the advisory of Engr. Cecil A. Cespedes was named after the Filipino Scientist Dr. Juan Salcedo Jr.

Initiative

The students of 11 – Salcedo created this project to present a sustainable and creative way of products that are made of recyclable materials.

How did we do this?

From planning, collecting materials, and polishing the exhibit, Salcedo exhibited their immense effort and teamwork.
